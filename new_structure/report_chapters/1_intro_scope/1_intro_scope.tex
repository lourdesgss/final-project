\section{Introduction and Scope}
This report presents a comprehensive security assessment of the FESTO Smart Factory, covering its current architecture, the threats and risks it faces, and a set of concrete mitigation measures. The analysis is grounded in a detailed inventory of the factory's hardware, software, and communication protocols, established through physical inspection of the installation, network traffic capture, and review of vendor and protocol documentation.

The scope of this report is limited to the security posture of the factory as observed during the assessment period; it does not extend to physical safety systems, production quality assurance, or components outside the network boundary defined in Section~\ref{sec:asis}. Findings are evaluated against recognised industrial security standards, and the proposed mitigations are scoped to be realistically implementable within the lab environment.
