\section{Standards and Regulatory Alignment}

The security requirements in Section~\ref{sec:secreq} were derived in alignment with three frameworks. \textbf{IEC 62443} provides the foundational requirement structure for industrial automation and control system (IACS) security and underlies the SR numbering used throughout this report. \textbf{ISO/IEC 27001} is applied where a requirement extends beyond purely OT-specific concerns into general information security management practice (access control, backup, logging). The \textbf{NIS2 Directive} is included to ground the requirements in the EU regulatory baseline applicable to operators of essential and important entities. Table~\ref{tab:standards-alignment} traces each security requirement back to its supporting standard or regulatory clause.

SR-01 & Network Segmentation &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Zone separation: OT Field, OT Cell, IDMZ, IT Zone
\item VLAN/subnet per zone
\item No direct IT $\rightarrow$ OT Field communication
\item Firewall-controlled conduits only
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-3-3 SR 5.1; IEC 62443-1-1; NIS2 Art.~21(2)(d) \\

SR-02 & Firewall with Protocol Inspection &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Industrial firewalls between IT and OT zones
\item Default-deny inter-zone policy
\item Allowed: OPC UA (4840), MQTT TLS (8883)
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-3-3 SR 5.2; IEC 62443-2-1 \\

SR-03 & OPC UA Hardening &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Sign \& Encrypt (Basic256Sha256)
\item Certificate-based authentication
\item Disable anonymous access
\item Access only via IDMZ OPC UA proxy
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1, 1.3, 3.1; OPC UA Part 2 \\

SR-04 & MQTT Hardening &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item TLS 1.2+ required
\item Client certificate authentication
\item Topic-level ACL enforcement
\item IT zone read-only access
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1, 3.1; MQTT v5; NIS2 Art.~21(2)(h) \\

SR-05 & HTTP/Web Service Hardening &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Disable unnecessary HTTP services
\item Enforce HTTPS (TLS 1.2+)
\item Device certificates required
\item Firewall-restricted access to specific systems
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1, 3.1; NIS2 Art.~21(2)(h) \\

SR-06 & Authentication and Least Privilege &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Individual authentication for all OT access
\item Role-based access control (Operator / Engineer / Admin)
\item Engineering access restricted to dedicated workstation
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1--1.3; IEC 62443-2-1 §4.3.3; ISO/IEC 27001 A.9 \\

SR-07 & Secure Engineering Access &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Isolated engineering workstation
\item Firewall-controlled PLC access only
\item TIA Portal restricted communication
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1, 5.2 \\

SR-08 & Logging and Monitoring &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Centralized syslog collection
\item Firewall logs all denied traffic
\item OPC UA authentication failure logging
\item MQTT connection event logging
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-3-3 SR 6.1, 6.2; ISO/IEC 27001 A.12.4; NIS2 Art.~21(2)(f) \\

SR-09 & Time Synchronization (NTP) &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Single authoritative NTP server
\item All OT devices synchronized
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-2-1 §4.3.4; ISO/IEC 27001 A.12.3; NIS2 Art.~21(2)(c) \\

SR-10 & Backup and Recovery &
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*, noitemsep, topsep=2pt]
\item Backups after configuration changes
\item Offline backup storage
\item Recovery procedure tested biannually
\item Daily MES database backup
\end{itemize}
&
IEC 62443-2-1 §4.3.4; ISO/IEC 27001 A.12.3; NIS2 Art.~21(2)(c) \\
