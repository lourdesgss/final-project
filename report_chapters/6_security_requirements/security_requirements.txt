SR-01 - Network Segmentation

sr-01.1 Network shall be divided into 5 zones:
    > OT field zone (PLC's, HMI's)
    > OT cell zone (Festo Controllers, Robot Controller, Camera, MQTT device)
    > IDMZ (OPC/UA Proxy, MQTT broker, backup server historian)
    > IT Zone (MES PC, COBOL PC)
sr-01.2 Each zone must be implemented on separate subnetwork with inter-zone traffic controlled by the firewall rules.
sr-01.3 No device from IT zone shall directly reach OT field zone.

sr-01 Standards reference:
    > IEC 62443-3-3 SR 5.1 (Network Segmentation)
    > IEC 62443-1-1 Zone & Conduit model
    > NIS2 Article 21(2)(d)

SR-02 - Firewall with industrial protocol inspection
The OT zones and IT zones shall be separated with the industrial firewall(s). 
Default-denay policy an all inter-zone conduits, with only explicitly
whitelisted flows permitted:
    > OPC UA (TCP port 4840) from IDMZ OPC UA proxy to PLC's and Festo Controllers
    > MQTT (TCP port 8883 TLS) from MQTT device to MQTT broker in IDMZ
    > HTTP (TCP 80) alarm pooling from OPC UA in IDMZ
    
Standards:
     > IEC 62443-3-3 SR 5.2
     > IEC 62443-2-1 security management
    
SR-03 - OPC UA Hardening
All OPC UA servers shall operate in Security Mode: Sign&Encrypt with SecurityPolicy:Basic256Sha256.
Each client (MES, COBOL) must authenticate with an application certificate. Annonymous and username/password authentication sessions shall be disabled.
OPC/UA servers shall not be reachable from the nodes inside IT zone directly, all access routes shall be set through a dedicated OPC UA proxy node.

Standards:
    > IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1, SR 1.3, SR 3.1; OPC UA Part 2 (Security Model)

SR-04 MQTT Hardening
The MQTT broker shall be hosted on a dedicated server inside IDMZ. All MQTT connections shall use TLS 1.2 or higher.
Each MQTT publusher (MQTT CXV device) shall authenticate with a client certificate.
Topic level ACLs must restrict publishers only to its own publish topics.
Nodes in IT zone shall only subscribe to the OT topics.

Standards:
    > IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1, SR 3.1; MQTT v5 specification; NIS2 Article 21(2)(h)

SR-05 HTTP/Web Interface Hardening
All HTTP services on PLC's HMI's, Festo Controllers, and cameras shall be disabled unless strictly required for an operational functionality.
If the Web Interface is required, it must be migrated to HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) with a device certificate, and access restricted by firewall to only sepcific source
IP's of pooling systems.
Shared/default passwords must be replaced with unique strong cridentials per device.
The MES Web Interface must enforce HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) and role-based access.

Standards:
    > IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1, SR 1.5;
    > NIST SP 800-82;
    > IEC 62443-2-1 patch/config management

SR-06 Authentication and Least Privilage
All interactive access (PLC programming, HMI configuration, robot controller configuration) to the OT devices shall require individual user authentication.
Role separation shall be enforced:
    > Operator (monitor only)
    > Engineer (upload programs, change parameters)
    > Administrator (device configuration, user management)

Furthermore, PLC Programming access (TIA Portal) shall be only possible from designated Engineering Workstation.

Standards:
    > IEC 62443-3-3 SR 1.1–1.3; 
    > IEC 62443-2-1 §4.3.3; 
    > ISO/IEC 27001 A.9

SR-04 Secure Engineering Access
A dedicated Engineering Workstation shall be physically or logically isolated from the IT Zone.
It shall connect to PLC's only through industrial firewall via controlled conduit, with firewall rules permitting only TIA Portal protocols to the PLC's IP's. 


SR-08 Logging and Monitoring
All network devices (managed switches, routers, industrial firewalls, PLC's with switch modules) shall send logs to a centralized Syslog collector in the IDMZ zone.
The industrial firewalls shall log all denied traffic.
OPC UA server shall log all sessions establishment and authentication failiures.
The MQTT brocker shall log all connect/disconnect events and authentication failiures.

Standards:
    > IEC 62443-3-3 SR 6.1, SR 6.2;
    > NIS2 Article 21(2)(f); 
    > ISO/IEC 27001 A.12.4

SR-09 NTP/Time synchronization
A single NTP server must be designated. All OT devices shall synchronize using this server.

Standards:
    > IEC 62443-2-1 §4.3.4; ISO/IEC 27001 A.12.3; NIS2 Article 21(2)(c)


SR-10 Backup and Recovery
All PLC programs, HMI and Controllers configurations must be backed up after every change to and dedicated backup storage. 
Backup must be stored offline.
Recovery procedure shall be documented and tested at least once per half a year.
MES database shall be backed up once a day.

Standards:
    > IEC 62443-2-1 §4.3.4; 
    > ISO/IEC 27001 A.12.3; 
    > NIS2 Article 21(2)(c)
